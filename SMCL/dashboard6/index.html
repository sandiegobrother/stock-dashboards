AI Liquid Cooling — $200B+ TAM by 2028
Core Differentiator
VERY HIGH
SMCI is the market leader in direct liquid cooling (DLC) for AI GPU clusters — the fastest-growing server architecture as AI accelerators generate more heat than air cooling can handle. Every NVIDIA GB200 NVL72 rack requires liquid cooling. SMCI's DLC expertise, established supply chain, and deployment track record with major hyperscalers gives it a 12-18 month head start over Dell and HPE in this specific product category.
02
Hyperscaler AI CapEx — $350B+ in 2026
Demand Driver
VERY HIGH
Combined AI infrastructure capex from AWS, Azure, Google, Meta exceeds $350B in 2026. SMCI participates in this wave as a primary server supplier — $12B in secured design wins last quarter. Revenue of $12.7B in a single quarter already demonstrates the scale. The question is not whether demand exists — it does. The question is whether SMCI can convert demand to profit at acceptable margins.
03
Margin Recovery — From 6.3% Back to 10%+
Re-Rating Catalyst
HIGH IMPACT
Management guided Q3 FY2026 margins will improve. Each 100bps of gross margin recovery on $40B revenue = $400M more gross profit = ~$300M after-tax = ~$0.50 additional EPS. A recovery from 6.3% to 10% gross margin would triple SMCI's EPS from ~$2.20 to ~$6+ — and re-rate the stock from 16x to $60-90 range. This is the single largest growth catalyst for SMCI/SMCL.
