Business Description & Moat
Company Overview

Sembcorp Marine is a global marine and offshore engineering group with capabilities spanning repairs, upgrades, new building, and conversion of offshore platforms and vessels. The company is expanding into floating offshore wind, LNG vessels, and clean energy marine infrastructure as the energy transition reshapes its end markets.

Competitive Moat

Sembcorp Marine's moat lies in its deep technical expertise in complex marine engineering, established shipyard infrastructure in Singapore and beyond, and long-term customer relationships with major oil & gas and energy companies. Its pivot to offshore wind and LNG positions it well for the energy transition.
