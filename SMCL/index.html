What Is SMCL?
ETF Overview

SMCL is the GraniteShares 2x Long SMCI Daily ETF — a single-stock leveraged ETF that seeks to deliver 200% of Super Micro Computer's (SMCI) daily price performance, before fees and expenses.

If SMCI rises 5% in a day, SMCL targets a +10% return. If SMCI falls 5%, SMCL targets a -10% return. The leverage resets daily, which means multi-day compounding effects can cause SMCL to significantly diverge from 2x SMCI's cumulative returns over time — particularly in volatile, sideways markets (volatility decay).

SMCL is designed for short-term tactical trades, not long-term investing. It is most commonly used by traders with a short-duration bullish conviction on SMCI who want amplified exposure without using margin.

Underlying Company — Super Micro Computer (SMCI)

Super Micro Computer (NASDAQ: SMCI) is a San Jose-based server and storage solutions manufacturer that has become a key beneficiary of the AI infrastructure buildout. SMCI specializes in high-density, liquid-cooled servers and rack-scale solutions for hyperscaler AI deployments.

Q2 FY2026 (Dec 2025): Revenue of $12.7B (+123% YoY), EPS of $0.69 (beat $0.46 est by 50%). AI infrastructure now represents over 90% of revenue. Full-year FY2026 guidance: at least $40B in revenue.

Key risk: Gross margins have compressed sharply — from 18% in FY2023 to just 6.3-6.4% in recent quarters — reflecting intense pricing pressure from hyperscalers and competition from Dell.

Key Risks of Holding SMCL
CRITICAL
Volatility Decay (Beta Slippage)
Daily rebalancing causes compounding losses in volatile/sideways markets. Holding SMCL for weeks or months in a choppy SMCI environment will produce returns well below 2x SMCI's period return — and potentially negative returns even if SMCI is flat.
HIGH
SMCI Margin Compression
SMCI gross margins collapsed from 18% to 6.3% over 3 years. Any further margin decline or guidance cut causes amplified -2x SMCL reaction. Q3 FY2026 guide: $12.3B revenue with "anticipated improvement" in margins — unproven.
HIGH
Accounting / Regulatory History
SMCI filed late financials in 2024 and faced a DOJ investigation. Regulatory risk is elevated vs peers. Any recurrence would be amplified 2x in SMCL.
MEDIUM
Nvidia & Dell Competition
Nvidia entering server solutions and Dell's aggressive pricing are structural margin headwinds. SMCI's edge in liquid cooling and custom rack design must be maintained.
LOW
ETF-Level Counterparty Risk
SMCL uses swaps/derivatives to achieve leverage. Counterparty failure is theoretically possible but GraniteShares maintains collateral buffers. AUM ~$50-100M.

Investment Verdict · SMCL · Speculative · Short-Term Tactical Only

SMCL is a tactical trading instrument, not an investment. For traders with short-duration (1-5 day) bullish conviction on SMCI — particularly around earnings catalysts — SMCL offers 2x amplified exposure. Q2 FY2026 was a strong beat ($12.7B vs $10.4B expected, EPS $0.69 vs $0.46), confirming AI server demand is real and accelerating. The bull case: SMCI margins recover to 8-10%+, $40B FY2026 guide is hit — SMCI re-rates to $50-60, SMCL 2x amplifies the move. The bear case: margins stay compressed at 6%, hyperscalers squeeze pricing, SMCI falls to $20-25 — SMCL amplifies the decline 2x. Not suitable for buy-and-hold.
