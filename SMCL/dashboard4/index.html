Valuation Verdict · SMCI Looks Cheap — But Margin Risk Justifies Discount

At 16x P/E and 0.55x EV/Revenue, SMCI looks optically cheap — the lowest valuation of any major AI infrastructure name. But the 6.3% gross margin justifies the discount: a commodity server manufacturer with no pricing power deserves a trough multiple. The bull case for SMCL requires believing margins recover to 10%+ (which would re-rate SMCI to $60-80) — the margin recovery is the entire valuation thesis. The catalyst: May 5, 2026 Q3 FY2026 earnings — if margins improve 200-300bps, SMCL could double from current levels.
