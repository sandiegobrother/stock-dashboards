Brand Assessment · Score 6/10

3 · Switching Costs
Switching Costs · Score 6/10
6
Large offshore engineering projects involve 3-7 year contracts with significant upfront engineering investment. Once Seatrium has designed and commenced fabrication for an FPSO or offshore wind installation vessel, switching to another yard would require restarting engineering (12-18 months + $50-200M in rework costs) and accepting significant delivery delays.

8 · Moat Verdict
