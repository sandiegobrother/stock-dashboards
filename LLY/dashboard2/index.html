Revenue Analysis
Eli Lilly's revenue transformation — from $28.3B (2021) to ~$58B (2025), a 105% increase — is driven by tirzepatide's unprecedented clinical performance. Mounjaro (diabetes) and Zepbound (obesity) generated combined revenue exceeding $22B in 2024. The 20%+ clinical superiority versus semaglutide in weight loss creates durable clinical preference. This is the most important new drug class since statins — and Lilly holds a dominant market position through the 2030s.
