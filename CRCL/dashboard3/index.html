3 · Switching Costs
Switching Costs · Score 7/10
7
Businesses that have integrated USDC into payment rails, DeFi protocols, or treasury operations face real switching costs. Replacing USDC with USDT or PYUSD requires updating smart contracts (for DeFi), rewriting payment APIs, re-educating counterparties, and losing the compliance certifications that specifically referenced USDC.

4 · Cost Advantage
Cost Advantage · Score 6/10
6
USDC issuance has near-zero marginal cost — the primary economics is interest earned on the $45B+ in reserve assets (Treasury bills, money market funds). At $45B circulation, even modest interest rates generate $2B+ in annual interest income on reserves — a pure scale moat where larger USDC circulation directly increases revenue.
