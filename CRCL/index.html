Business Description & Moat
Company Overview

Circle is the issuer of USDC, the world's second-largest stablecoin with $50B+ in circulation. Circle earns net interest income on the reserves backing USDC and generates transaction and API fee revenue. The company recently went public and benefits from the growing adoption of stablecoins for payments, DeFi, and cross-border transfers.

Competitive Moat

Circle's moat is regulatory trust and institutional relationships. USDC is the preferred stablecoin for institutional DeFi, regulated exchanges, and enterprise payments. Circle holds all reserves in short-duration US Treasuries, making it the safest and most transparent dollar-backed stablecoin — a key differentiator from Tether.
