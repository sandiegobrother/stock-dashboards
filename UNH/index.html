Business Description & Moat
Company Overview

UnitedHealth Group is the largest US health insurer and one of the world's largest healthcare companies, operating through UnitedHealthcare (insurance) and Optum (health services, pharmacy benefits, analytics). The company covers 50M+ Americans and processed $1T+ in healthcare spending. UNH is navigating the aftermath of the Change Healthcare cyberattack and heightened political scrutiny of insurance industry practices.

Competitive Moat

UNH's moat is the Optum vertical integration flywheel — it simultaneously insures patients, manages their pharmacy benefits, provides their primary care, analyzes their data, and processes their claims. This creates network effects and switching costs that pure-play insurers cannot match. At 13x forward earnings, UNH is historically cheap relative to its earnings quality.
