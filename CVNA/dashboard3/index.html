8 · Moat Verdict
Competitive Advantage Verdict · CVNA · Overall Moat Score: 6/10 · Narrow Moat
Carvana holds a narrow but real moat built on brand pioneer status, AI-enabled operational efficiency, and the 7-day return guarantee. The moat is operational rather than structural — competitors can theoretically replicate the model, but execution at Carvana's scale with its AI investment is non-trivial. Moat Score: 6/10.
