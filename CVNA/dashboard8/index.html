Results Commentary
Carvana's Q4 2025 results confirmed the turnaround is real and sustainable. Full-year FY2025 adj. EBITDA of $2.23B and 596K units (+43% YoY) represents one of the most dramatic operational recoveries in retail history — from near-bankruptcy in 2022 to best-in-class EBITDA margins. The 11.3% adj. EBITDA margin is now materially above CarMax's 4% and AutoNation's 3%, validating the AI-powered operations thesis.
2025 demonstrated that our model works at scale. The combination of AI-powered reconditioning, dynamic pricing, and national inventory creates a structural cost advantage that competitors cannot replicate quickly.
— Ernie Garcia III, CEO, Q4 2025 Earnings Call
