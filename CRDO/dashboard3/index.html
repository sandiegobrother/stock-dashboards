3 · Switching Costs
Switching Costs · Score 8/10
8
AEC integration into hyperscaler AI cluster architectures creates deep switching costs — the entire cluster design assumes AEC's specific electrical characteristics (reach, power, form factor). Replacing AECs with optical transceivers or copper passive cables would require redesigning server rack architecture, changing switch ports, and modifying cooling infrastructure — a cluster-level redesign costing $50M+ per data center.
