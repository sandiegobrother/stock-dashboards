Results Commentary
lululemon's Q4 FY2025 was the third consecutive quarter of US comp sales pressure, with comparable sales growth of -1% in Americas vs +2% expected. China at +46% YoY (beating the +40% estimate) remained the one bright spot that confirms global brand health. The headline positive was the simultaneous announcement of a new CEO with premium retail and international expansion credentials — the market reaction recovered from initial -8% to +5% intraday on the CEO news.

Key Metrics Analysis
US comparable sales of -1% (vs +2% estimate) is the headline miss — the third consecutive quarter below +3% historical norm. Gross margin compression of -30bps to 57.2% reflects competitive pricing pressure from Alo Yoga and Vuori in the US market. China +46% (beating +40% estimate) is the counter-narrative that keeps institutional bulls engaged. Zero debt and $1.5B cash mean the company has full financial flexibility to invest in the US brand reset.
