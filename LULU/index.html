Bottom Line · HOLD · Target $337.00
Lululemon is a world-class brand at an inflection point — the US market is maturing while international, particularly China, is in the early stages of a multi-decade expansion. At 22x forward earnings the valuation is now reasonable after a significant derating from peak multiples. At 16% upside to consensus the stock is a Hold for current owners and interesting for new investors on any China growth acceleration. Hold. Target: $337.
