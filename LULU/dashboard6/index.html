China Expansion — 46% Growth and 500+ Store Path
Market Expansion
VERY HIGH
China growing +46% YoY is lululemon's primary growth engine — with only ~100 stores in mainland China vs 650+ in North America, the store expansion runway is 5-10 years. The Chinese wellness consumer is aspirationally aligned with lululemon's positioning. Target of 500+ China stores by 2030 would make China 25%+ of total revenue.

New CEO Product Innovation — Brand Reset
New Products
HIGH

Growth Verdict
Growth Potential Verdict · LULU · +8% → +12% Est. Revenue Growth 2-4 Years
lululemon's growth potential is solid and undervalued at current prices. Revenue growing from $10.6B to $15B+ (42% cumulative) driven primarily by China expansion and new CEO product innovation. At 22x P/E (10-year low) with zero debt and $2B FCF, the margin of safety is high for a brand with 57% gross margins and a 20-year premium positioning track record. China +46% growth is the clearest near-term indicator that the brand's global resonance is intact despite US competitive pressure.
