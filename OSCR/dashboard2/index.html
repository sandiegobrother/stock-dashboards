Revenue Analysis
Oscar Health's revenue grew from $3.5B (2021) to ~$9.5B (2025) — nearly tripling in four years. Growth is driven by record ACA marketplace enrollment (22M+ nationally) and Oscar's technology platform delivering lower admin costs. The Cigna technology partnership monetizes Oscar's platform through licensing. First full-year GAAP profitability in 2024 was the pivotal validation that the tech-driven health insurance model works at scale.
