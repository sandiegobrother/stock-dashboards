Long-Term Investment Thesis · OSCR · Portfolio Manager Perspective
Oscar is fairly valued for its core ACA insurance business but potentially significantly undervalued if the Cigna technology licensing platform scales. The 14x forward P/E with GAAP profitability and $2.5B cash is reasonable. The ACA subsidy binary is the primary risk. For institutions who believe ACA subsidies are extended (most likely scenario) and Cigna licensing reaches $500M+, Oscar is a 2-3x opportunity. For institutions who weight ACA non-renewal as >40% probability, the downside from current prices is meaningful.
