Growth Potential Verdict · OSCR · +15% → +20% Est. Revenue Growth 2-4 Years
Oscar's growth potential is solid and ACA-subsidy dependent. Revenue growing from $10.1B to $16B+ (58% cumulative) with technology-driven margin expansion. The Cigna technology licensing platform is the most underappreciated growth driver — if Oscar's technology processes claims for Cigna's 20M+ members, it transforms from an insurance company to a healthcare technology platform. ACA subsidy extension is the key binary for near-term enrollment growth.
