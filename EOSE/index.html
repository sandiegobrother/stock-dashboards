Analyst Verdict
Bottom Line · BUY · Target $8.00
Eos Energy represents one of the highest-risk, highest-reward opportunities on this list. Q4 2025 revenue of $58M exceeded prior three quarters combined, $624M cash removes the existential risk, and the $300-400M FY2026 guidance implies extraordinary growth. The problem is the gross margin: -$54M gross loss on $58M revenue means the company is paying more to make batteries than it receives. The unit economics must improve dramatically. The 39% post-earnings stock drop reflects this concern. For investors who believe in the long-duration storage secular trend and Eos's zinc chemistry advantage, the $8 target and $15 bull case offer compelling asymmetry — but this is strictly speculative. Speculative Buy. Target: $8.00. High binary risk. Small position only.
