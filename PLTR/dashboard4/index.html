P/E Assessment
Palantir's ~80x forward P/E is 2-3x the enterprise software average of 35x — the market is pricing in sustained 50%+ revenue growth for 5+ years, which is an extraordinarily high bar even for AIP's demonstrated momentum. The FY2026 revenue guidance of $7.19B (+61% YoY) crushing consensus by $1B is the strongest justification for the premium. However, at 80x earnings, Palantir requires near-perfect execution — any slowdown in AIP adoption rate would result in severe multiple compression. The Rule-of-40 score of 127% is world-class; the question is whether 80x earnings is the right price for it.
