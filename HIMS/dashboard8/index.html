Results Commentary
Hims & Hers Q4 2025 showed the business is managing the FDA compounding transition better than feared, while the Novo Nordisk partnership begins contributing to revenue. Revenue of $623M (+76% YoY, +7.4% vs estimate) and the FY2026 guide of $3.2-3.5B (+12% above consensus) were the two most important positive surprises. The 79.5% gross margin (+150bps) confirms the telehealth platform economics are strengthening as the business scales.
Our partnership with Novo Nordisk positions Hims & Hers as the premier DTC channel for branded GLP-1 medications. We are uniquely positioned to serve the millions of Americans seeking convenient, affordable access to these transformative treatments.
— Andrew Dudum, CEO, Q4 2025 Earnings Call

Key Metrics Analysis
Gross margin of 79.5% (+150bps vs estimate) is the quality metric that matters most — at ~80% gross margins, Hims has achieved software-like healthcare economics on a $2.35B revenue base. The 2.2M subscribers (4.8% beat) combined with +5.9% revenue per subscriber proves both volume and pricing power are growing simultaneously. The FDA compounding 'managed better than feared' commentary relieved institutional investors who had feared a revenue cliff.
