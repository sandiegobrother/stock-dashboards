01
AI Training and Inference Storage — Enterprise SSD
Market Expansion
VERY HIGH
AI model training requires massive high-speed storage — GPT-4 class models require 100+ petabytes of storage for training data. Enterprise SSD (Sandisk's strength at 56% of revenue) is the fastest-growing NAND segment at 30%+/yr. AI inference (running models at scale) requires even faster storage for KV cache. Enterprise SSD price per TB is 3-5x commodity NAND, creating margin expansion.

Industry Growth Context
NAND flash storage is in a recovery cycle after the 2022-2023 oversupply correction — and AI data center demand is creating a new structural demand floor that prevents the next severe oversupply. Enterprise SSD is the highest-value NAND segment and is growing 30%+ annually. Sandisk's pure-play NAND focus allows management to optimize specifically for this opportunity vs the conglomerate structure of Western Digital.
