Business Description & Moat
Company Overview

SanDisk is a newly independent NAND flash memory company spun off from Western Digital. It designs and manufactures NAND flash storage products including SSDs, memory cards, and flash components. SanDisk benefits from the AI-driven surge in data storage demand as AI training datasets, inference, and storage requirements grow exponentially.

Competitive Moat

SanDisk's moat is its manufacturing scale and joint venture relationship with Kioxia, providing cost-competitive NAND production. Brand recognition in consumer and enterprise SSD markets is strong. AI data growth is a secular tailwind — every AI model trained and every inference run requires massive storage infrastructure.
