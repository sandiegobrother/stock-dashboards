8 · Moat Verdict
Competitive Advantage Verdict · MSTR · Overall Moat Score: 6/10 · Narrow Moat
Strategy holds a unique but leverage-dependent moat as the world's largest corporate Bitcoin treasury. The $21/42 capital model is genuinely innovative financial engineering, and 738,731 BTC creates institutional validation effects that smaller corporate buyers cannot replicate. The moat is real but fragile — entirely contingent on Bitcoin price levels remaining above the $8.2B debt's effective liquidation threshold. Moat Score: 6/10. Narrow Moat. BTC treasury pioneer + $21/42 model are novel advantages; leverage risk limits moat durability confidence.
