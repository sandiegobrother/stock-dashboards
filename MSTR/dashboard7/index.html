Why Institutions Avoid
A1
Single Asset Concentration — 100% BTC Price Risk
CONCENTRATION
Strategy's investment value is 100% correlated to Bitcoin price — a correlation that is unacceptable for diversification-mandated institutional portfolios. Pension funds, endowments, and insurance companies with diversification requirements cannot meaningfully allocate to a single-asset holding company. Most institutional mandates cap crypto exposure at 1-2% — making MSTR a small speculative allocation at best.

SENTIMENT

Long-Term Investment Thesis · MSTR · Portfolio Manager Perspective

The thesis is binary and simple: if you believe Bitcoin will be worth significantly more than $82K in 3-5 years, Strategy provides 1.6x leveraged exposure with $477M in software revenue covering operations.
