Industry Growth Context
Latin America is one of the world's fastest-growing digital economies — 650M people, 70%+ smartphone penetration, and structural tailwinds from financial inclusion (50%+ previously unbanked now using Mercado Pago). The combination of Amazon (e-commerce), PayPal (payments), Square (SMB financial services), and Shopify (e-commerce tools) in one platform is uniquely valuable in a market where these functions don't exist separately.

Growth Verdict
Growth Potential Verdict · MELI · +25% → +30% Est. Revenue Growth 2-4 Years
MercadoLibre has one of the most compelling long-term growth stories globally. Revenue growing from $21B to $42B+ (100% cumulative over 3 years) in the world's most underpenetrated major digital economy is driven by secular tailwinds that will persist for 15-20 years. The $1B buyback and PEG of 0.7 confirm management and the market both see the stock as undervalued. The Shopee competitive threat is real but manageable given MELI's 25-year first-mover moat.
