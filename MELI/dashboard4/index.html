P/E Assessment
MercadoLibre's P/E has compressed from 55x to 28x as Shopee competition and margin pressure triggered a JPMorgan downgrade and broader investor concern. At 28x forward P/E for a company growing revenue 39% with 650M people and 15% e-commerce penetration, this is historically cheap for MELI. The $1B buyback authorization signals management's own assessment that the stock is significantly undervalued. EPS growth has been extraordinary: from $14.82 (2022) to ~$59 (2025) — a 300% increase in three years.

DCF Analysis
The base DCF of $2,200 implies 33% upside. The bull case $3,500 requires 35%+ FCF growth through 2030 as Mercado Pago credit portfolio scales — achievable given 78M MAU growing 25% annually. The bear case $1,200 requires Shopee to structurally capture 25%+ of MELI's GMV — a dramatic outcome given MELI's 25-year brand and logistics moat. At 28x P/E for the Amazon + PayPal + Shopify of Latin America, the Shopee discount appears excessive.
