Results Commentary
MercadoLibre's Q4 2025 demonstrated the continued strength of the LatAm digital economy despite Shopee competition and FX headwinds. Revenue of $6.1B grew +39% YoY with GMV growing +28% and Fintech +45% — both segments accelerating. The first-ever $1B share buyback was the most significant management signal: at 28x forward P/E for 39% growth, management explicitly believes the stock is undervalued. The Shopee competition concern has not materialized as the structural impairment bears feared.
We ended 2025 with strong momentum across commerce and fintech. Our $1B buyback authorization reflects our belief that MELI shares represent a compelling value at current prices.
— Osvaldo Gimenez, CEO, Q4 2025 Earnings Call
