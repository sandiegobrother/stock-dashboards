Revenue Analysis
TransMedics built a unique integrated organ transplant platform — the Organ Care System (OCS) device combined with the National OCS Program (NOP) aviation logistics. Revenue grew from $88M to $605.5M in four years — a 589% increase. The 22-aircraft owned fleet creates a vertical integration moat. Every organ transplanted via OCS saves a life on the 100,000-person transplant waitlist — the product-market fit is as strong as it gets in medical devices.
