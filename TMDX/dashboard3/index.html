3 · Switching Costs
Switching Costs · Score 9/10
9
Switching costs are exceptionally high — transplant centers that have built organ procurement protocols around NOP and trained surgical teams on OCS face complete protocol rebuilds and surgeon retraining for life-critical procedures. The life-or-death nature of transplant surgery makes switching vendor mid-protocol an extraordinary clinical risk that surgical teams and hospital administrators are extremely reluctant to accept.
