Industry Growth Context
TransMedics has created a new category in medicine — the integrated organ transplant system company. Before OCS, organs were transported in coolers with severe viability constraints; now they're transported in perfusion machines on private jets with clinical teams. This is not incremental improvement — it's a category creation that fundamentally changes transplant medicine economics and patient outcomes.

Growth Verdict
Growth Potential Verdict · TMDX · +22% → +30% Est. Revenue Growth 2-4 Years
TransMedics has exceptional growth potential anchored in kidney OCS. Revenue growing from $605M to $1.3B+ (115% cumulative in 3 years) — and the kidney OCS approval is not even needed to justify the base case. If kidney OCS is approved, add $400M+ in peak annual US revenue alone — making the $1.3B FY2028E estimate potentially conservative. The 22-jet aviation moat creates barriers to competition that no pure-play medical device company can easily replicate.
