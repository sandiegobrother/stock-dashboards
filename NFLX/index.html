Analyst Verdict
Bottom Line · BUY · Target $1,100.00
Netflix has successfully transitioned from growth-at-all-costs to a disciplined, profitable streaming giant with multiple compounding growth vectors. The advertising business, live sports strategy, and global penetration represent durable revenue growth levers. The password sharing crackdown is complete and converted — that tailwind is now fully annualized. At 12% upside to consensus the stock is fairly valued but not cheap, making it a quality compounder for long-term portfolios rather than a high-conviction near-term trade. Buy. Target: $1,100. Bull case $1,400 if advertising scales faster than expected.
