Net Income Analysis

Is Netflix, Inc. Financially Strong or Weakening? — Definitive Assessment
Netflix is financially strong and improving across every metric. The recovery from the 2022 subscriber crisis — driven by the password crackdown and advertising tier — was decisive. FCF of $8B, 30% operating margin, and 301M+ subscribers make Netflix a durable entertainment franchise. The risk is content cost discipline and sustaining ARPU growth as subscriber growth moderates. Financial health: A. Trend: Strengthening. One of the cleanest media company financial profiles ever built.
