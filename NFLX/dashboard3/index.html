1 · Brand Strength
Brand Assessment · Score 9/10
9
Netflix defined streaming globally — its name is used as a verb. With 301M+ subscribers across 190 countries, Netflix has global brand recognition that Disney+, Max, and Apple TV+ cannot match at the same international scale. Live sports expansion (NFL, WWE) adds event-driven cultural relevance that pure-VOD competitors lack.

Network Effects · Score 6/10

'Have you seen Squid Game?' — Netflix originals create global cultural conversations that drive subscriber growth at zero marginal cost. The ad tier creates a nascent advertiser network loop.

3 · Switching Costs
Switching Costs · Score 6/10
6
Netflix's switching costs are primarily content depth (15,000+ titles means always something new) and live sports exclusivity. NFL Thursday Night Football and WWE Raw are exclusively on Netflix — a subscriber who wants these programs cannot switch providers. The recommendation engine personalization further deepens the 'you specifically' content lock-in over time.

4 · Cost Advantage
Cost Advantage · Score 8/10
8
$17B content spend spread across 301M subscribers gives Netflix the lowest per-subscriber content cost in streaming. Disney+ ($8B, 120M subs), Apple TV+ ($7B, 25M subs) each spend more per subscriber than Netflix. 47% gross margin expanding toward 50% reflects operating leverage of global streaming infrastructure.

5 · Patents & Proprietary Technology
IP & Proprietary Tech · Score 7/10
7
Netflix owns globally recognized original IP outright — Stranger Things, Squid Game, Bridgerton, Money Heist are $1B+ franchises. Unlike studio films shared with talent, Netflix owns these IP assets 100% — merchandise, sequels, theme parks, and gaming are all Netflix's to monetize. The recommendation algorithm trained on 300M+ user profiles is a proprietary asset impossible to replicate without the user base.

8 · Moat Verdict
Competitive Advantage Verdict · NFLX · Overall Moat Score: 7/10 · Narrow-Wide Moat
Netflix holds a genuine but not impregnable moat built on content scale advantage, 301M subscriber recommendation algorithm, and globally recognized original IP. The addition of NFL and WWE creates appointment viewing unavailable elsewhere. The weakness relative to Disney is IP library depth (Marvel/Star Wars franchise power). Moat Score: 7/10. Narrow-Wide Moat. Content scale + global reach + live sports are durable; Disney IP depth is the relative weakness.
