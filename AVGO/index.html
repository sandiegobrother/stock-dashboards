Analyst Verdict
Bottom Line — Strong Buy · Target $467.68
Broadcom is one of the most asymmetric risk/reward opportunities in large-cap technology today. It sits at the intersection of the two most powerful infrastructure trends of the decade — AI compute and enterprise cloud migration — with a near-monopoly position in custom AI silicon (~70% share) and a growing, recurring high-margin software franchise from VMware. The 0.64 PEG ratio and 45%+ analyst consensus upside suggest the market has not fully priced in FY2027 earnings power. The Q2 FY2026 guidance of $22.0B (+47% YoY) further validates the trajectory. Q1 FCF of $8.0B alone annualises to a ~4.9% FCF yield on today's market cap — exceptional for a company growing this fast. Primary downside risk is customer concentration, not competitive displacement. Maintain Strong Buy. Price Target: $467.68. Upside to $630 in bull scenario.
