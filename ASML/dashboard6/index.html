ASML 2025 Investor Day
Key Growth Drivers
01
High-NA EUV ASP Escalation — €200M → €400M Per System
New Products
VERY HIGH
High-NA EUV systems (EXE:5200B) at €400M each are transitioning from pilot to volume production in 2025-2026. The ASP doubling from standard EUV (€200M) means each High-NA system replaced generates twice the revenue. With 50-80 High-NA systems targeted annually by 2027, this single product transition could add €8-15B in annual incremental revenue. SK Hynix, Samsung, and Intel are in active deployment — TSMC's commitment would be a step-change catalyst.
02
IBM Installed Base Compounding — 8,000+ Systems → 12,000+ by 2028
Market Expansion
VERY HIGH
Every ASML system sold today generates 20-30 years of IBM service revenue. The installed base grows with each system shipped — and IBM revenue per system also grows through upgrades, field options, and software expansions. IBM grew +26% in FY2025 to €8.2B and is on track for €12B+ by 2028. At 60%+ gross margins, IBM is ASML's highest-quality revenue stream and partially recession-proof (customers must service existing systems even during CapEx freezes).
03
AI Chip Demand Driving Logic CapEx — TSMC, Samsung, SK Hynix
AI Technology
HIGH
Every AI GPU (NVIDIA Blackwell, AMD MI300), every HBM memory chip, every smartphone SoC requires EUV lithography. TSMC is investing $165B+ in new fab capacity through 2028 — the largest semiconductor capex commitment in history. Samsung's HBM4 ramp for AI memory requires High-NA EUV. Micron's AI DRAM expansion is adding EUV capacity in the US and Japan. Logic revenue grew +22% YoY in FY2025 to €16.1B. AI is the structural demand driver ASML's long-term growth model depends upon.
04
Sovereign Semiconductor Programs — CHIPS Act, EU Chips Act, Japan
Market Expansion
HIGH
17 new semiconductor fabs are planned or under construction in the US alone — all requiring ASML EUV systems. The EU Chips Act targets 20% of global semiconductor manufacturing in Europe. Japan's Rapidus (2nm target) is an ASML customer. These sovereign programs represent government-backed, non-cyclical demand that is policy-protected from normal semiconductor CapEx discipline. US revenue grew +11% in FY2025 and is accelerating as TSMC Arizona, Samsung Texas, and Intel Ohio ramp.

Growth Verdict · ASML · +50-70% Revenue Growth 2025-2028 · Backlog-Anchored

ASML's 2-4 year growth story is anchored in the €38.8B backlog — the most contracted forward revenue in semiconductor equipment history. FY2026 guidance of €34-39B and the Investor Day FY2028 target of €44-60B (midpoint ~€52B) represent 60-70% cumulative revenue growth from FY2025. The High-NA ASP doubling is the multiplicative driver: 50-80 systems × €400M = €20-32B of High-NA revenue alone by FY2027-2028. IBM compounding to €12B+ adds the annuity layer. Bernstein's $1,911 price target explicitly models this trajectory.
