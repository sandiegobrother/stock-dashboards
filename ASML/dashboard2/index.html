Financial Assessment · ASML · Fortress Balance Sheet + Margin Expansion

ASML's financial profile is exceptional for an industrial company — 52.8% gross margins (software-like), €11B FCF on €32.7B revenue (34% FCF margin), zero net debt, and €13.3B cash. The FY2025 EPS of €24.73 (+28.5% YoY) is growing significantly faster than revenue, reflecting gross margin expansion and operating leverage. The €38.8B backlog provides 14+ months of revenue visibility — unusual certainty for a capital equipment supplier. IBM segment (€8.2B, +26%) is the highest-quality recurring revenue engine, growing faster than system sales and carrying 60%+ gross margins.
