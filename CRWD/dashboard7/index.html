Why Institutions Buy
B1
1T Events/Day Data Flywheel — Compounding Threat Intelligence
MOAT
CrowdStrike's Threat Graph processes 1 trillion security events per day — the largest real-time threat intelligence dataset in cybersecurity. Every new customer improves detection accuracy for all customers. This data flywheel creates compounding detection superiority that no competitor with less scale can match. Institutional technology investors see this as the most durable moat in enterprise security.
B2
July 2024 Outage Recovery — Q4 FY2026 Net New ARR +47% YoY
RESILIENCE
After the July 2024 IT outage caused 8.5M Windows crashes, institutional investors who stayed in or added were rewarded — Q4 FY2026 Net New ARR grew +47% YoY, confirming the moat held. A company whose customers stay and renew after the worst IT incident in history has the most validated customer stickiness in enterprise software. This resilience test is now a permanent bull argument.
B3
Platform Consolidation — 25+ Modules vs 5-7 Point Solutions
GROWTH
Enterprises are consolidating security vendors from 5-7 point solutions to unified platforms — CrowdStrike Falcon with 25+ modules is the primary beneficiary. Each customer adding endpoint + cloud + identity + LogScale reduces 3-4 separate vendor relationships. Module expansion drives NRR above 120% and makes Falcon increasingly mission-critical.
Why Institutions Avoid
A1
55x Forward P/E — Premium to Cybersecurity Peers
VALUATION
CrowdStrike at 55x forward P/E trades above sector average (45x) and above Palo Alto Networks (40x) despite comparable growth rates. The premium reflects the July 2024 resilience narrative and Threat Graph superiority — but GARP investors question whether 55x is justified for 22% ARR growth. Base DCF is approximately $380 — essentially in line with current prices — implying minimal upside in the base case.
A2
Microsoft Defender Bundled Competition
COMPETITION
Microsoft bundles Defender for Endpoint with M365/Azure at no incremental cost — the most dangerous competitive dynamic in enterprise software. In SMB and mid-market segments, procurement teams increasingly opt for 'good enough' free security rather than paying CrowdStrike's premium. Market share pressure in the down-market could impair future land-and-expand conversion funnels.
A3
Outstanding July 2024 Legal Liability
LEGAL RISK
Delta Air Lines' $380M+ lawsuit and multiple class actions from the July 2024 outage remain unresolved. Institutional legal and compliance officers flag the open litigation as a position sizing constraint. Final liability could range from $200M (manageable) to $2B+ (EPS impairment).
