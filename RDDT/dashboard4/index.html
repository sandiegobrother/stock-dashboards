P/E Assessment
Reddit's P/E compressed dramatically as EPS caught up — GAAP NI grew from $179M (2024) to $709M (2025), a quadrupling in one year. At 55x forward P/E for a business with 90% gross margins, $684M FCF, $1B buyback, and 18+ years of authentic human conversation data that AI companies are licensing, the multiple is defensible. The key valuation debate is whether AI data licensing revenue is structurally durable — if yes, Reddit deserves 60-80x on growing earnings; if AI-generated content eventually displaces human content value, the multiple compresses.
