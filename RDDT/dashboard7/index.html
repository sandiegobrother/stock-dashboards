Why Institutions Buy
B1
18-Year Authentic Data Moat — AI Training Premium in the Synthetic Data Era
UNIQUE ASSET
Reddit's 18+ years of authentic human conversation across 100,000+ specialized communities is the most valuable pre-AI data asset in existence — and grows more valuable as AI-generated content floods the internet. The paradox: as AI makes synthetic content cheaper, authentic human-generated content becomes MORE valuable for training future AI models. Reddit is the paradoxical winner of AI proliferation.
B2
90% Gross Margin + GAAP Profitability — Software Economics
QUALITY
Reddit's 90% gross margin and first-ever GAAP profitability (FY2025 GAAP NI $709M) demonstrate that the platform has achieved genuine software economics. At 301M DAU with 90% margins, each incremental user and advertiser is nearly pure profit. Institutional software investors find this margin profile exceptional for a consumer internet business.
B3
$1B Buyback — Management Signals Undervaluation at 55x P/E
INSIDER SIGNAL
Reddit authorizing a $1B buyback at current prices (55x forward P/E) is the most powerful insider signal available. A company with a 1-year IPO history buying back stock at 55x earnings is telling institutions: we believe our earnings will grow so rapidly that 55x is actually cheap on a 2-3 year basis. Reddit Answers and AI licensing give management visibility to justify this conviction.
Why Institutions Avoid
A1
55x P/E with 90% Ad Revenue Concentration
VALUATION + RISK
55x forward P/E for a business with 90% advertising concentration is a premium that requires sustained 40%+ growth. Any advertising market slowdown (recession) hits Reddit disproportionately vs diversified platforms. Value and GARP investors find the 55x premium unjustifiable for the ad market exposure.
A2
4.2% Short Interest — Short-Seller Skepticism
SENTIMENT
Reddit's 4.2% short interest is elevated — reflecting both the high valuation and skepticism about AI data licensing revenue durability. Bears argue that AI-generated synthetic data will eventually substitute for Reddit's authentic data, reducing licensing premiums over time.

Investment Thesis
Long-Term Investment Thesis · RDDT · Portfolio Manager Perspective
Reddit is the most uniquely positioned social platform for the AI era: it owns the authentic human conversation dataset that AI companies need most and that becomes more valuable as the internet fills with synthetic content. The 90% gross margins reflect true platform economics — 18 years of community investment has created barriers to competition that no funded startup can quickly replicate. The $1B buyback at 55x P/E is the strongest management conviction signal since the IPO. The AI data licensing (Google $60M/yr, OpenAI undisclosed) is the new revenue category that consensus models undervalue because it's opaque and early-stage.
