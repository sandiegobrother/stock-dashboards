Results Commentary
Reddit's Q4 2025 marked a transformational quarter — GAAP net income of $229M for Q4 and $709M for full-year 2025 confirmed the platform has definitively achieved software economics. Data licensing revenue of $39M (+120% YoY, +30% vs estimate) is the clearest signal that Reddit's AI data moat is being monetized at an accelerating pace. The $1B buyback authorization at 55x P/E signals management's conviction that earnings will compound rapidly from here.

Key Metrics Analysis
The data licensing revenue of $39M (+30% vs $30M estimate) is the most important growth signal — it's the new revenue category that makes Reddit's AI era positioning unique among social platforms. DAU of 101.7M (+39% YoY) confirms engagement is accelerating, not plateauing. International revenue +85% YoY (vs +70% expected) shows the monetization gap between US and international users is narrowing. The 51.6% adj. EBITDA margin is the highest in Reddit's public history.
