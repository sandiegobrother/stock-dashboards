Industry Growth Context
CAVA is following the Chipotle playbook almost exactly — starting in East Coast cities, expanding methodically, achieving AUV above sector average, and only now reaching the scale where operating leverage kicks in. Chipotle grew from 500 stores to 3,500 over 15 years with extraordinary returns. CAVA at 439 stores is at the equivalent of Chipotle circa 2007 — the growth runway is multi-decade.

Growth Verdict
Growth Potential Verdict · CAVA · +20% → +25% Est. Revenue Growth 2-4 Years
CAVA's growth potential is outstanding for patient investors. Revenue growing from $1.05B to $2.3B+ (119% cumulative in 3 years) following the proven Chipotle model. +10.8% same-store sales growth is the most important indicator — it confirms the brand is accelerating, not plateauing, as unit count grows.
