3 · Switching Costs
Switching Costs · Score 10/10
10
Oracle database switching costs are among the highest in enterprise software history. A Fortune 500 company that has run Oracle Database for 20+ years faces re-engineering every application layer, retraining thousands of DBAs, migrating petabytes of data, and risking production downtime during transition — typically a $50-500M+ multi-year project. The Oracle ERP switching cost is equally severe: 5-10 year SAP-to-Oracle or Oracle-to-SAP migrations are notoriously expensive and risky.
