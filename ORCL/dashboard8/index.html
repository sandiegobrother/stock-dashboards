Results Commentary
Oracle's Q3 FY2026 demonstrated that the Stargate partnership and OCI investment cycle is generating tangible results. OCI grew +84% YoY — the fastest quarter in Oracle cloud history — and the RPO of $553B (+325% YoY) remains the most contracted revenue pipeline in enterprise software. The non-GAAP operating margin of 45.5% (+150bps) was the positive surprise — despite massive CapEx, Oracle is maintaining software-like margins in its cloud business.
The demand for our cloud infrastructure is unprecedented. AI and data workloads are driving multi-year capacity commitments from hyperscalers, governments, and enterprises globally.
— Larry Ellison, Chairman, Q3 FY2026 Earnings Call

Key Metrics Analysis
The $553B RPO total (including $130B in short-term obligations) is the defining Oracle metric — it represents 8+ years of contracted revenue at current conversion rates. OCI +84% confirms the Stargate AI partnership is generating real revenue acceleration. The CapEx of $3.2B (higher than $3.0B estimate) confirms Oracle is investing aggressively — negative near-term FCF but building the infrastructure for the $66B+ FY2027 target.
