Is Oracle Corporation Financially Strong or Weakening? — Definitive Assessment
Oracle is in a financial transformation — accelerating revenue growth driven by OCI's 84% expansion, but temporarily negative FCF due to $48B in data center CapEx. The $553B RPO growing 325% YoY is the clearest forward indicator of future revenue. The $94B debt load is the primary near-term financial risk — it is elevated but manageable if OCI revenues convert. Financial health: B+. Trend: Revenue strongly improving; FCF recovery required. High conviction in OCI growth required to be comfortable with the leverage.
