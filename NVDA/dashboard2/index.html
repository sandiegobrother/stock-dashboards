Revenue Analysis
NVIDIA's revenue trajectory is unprecedented for a semiconductor company at this scale. From $26.9B (FY2022) to an estimated $215.9B (FY2026) — an 8x increase in four years driven by the AI GPU supercycle. Data center revenue alone reached $115B+ in FY2025. The Hopper (H100/H200) architecture captured essentially all major AI training demand from 2023, while Blackwell (B200/GB200/B300) continues to dominate into 2026. No other semiconductor company has ever generated this level of revenue growth at this scale.
