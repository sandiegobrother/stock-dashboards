P/E Assessment
NVIDIA's forward P/E of 22x is 21% below the semiconductor industry median of 29x — remarkable for the company generating 80% of AI GPU revenue. The TTM P/E of 37x reflects rapidly expanding earnings outpacing the stock price, while the 5-year average P/E of 69x shows how much multiple compression has occurred as EPS caught up to the narrative. At a PEG ratio of 0.57, NVIDIA is statistically cheap on a growth-adjusted basis. The forward P/E discounts ~$216B in FY2026E revenue and ~$8.25 in EPS — both achievable given Q4 FY2026 guidance of $43B quarterly revenue.
