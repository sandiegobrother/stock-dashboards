VALIDATION

B2

DEMAND
$20B in committed customer backlog provides the revenue foundation for the $7-9B ARR target. Unlike speculative pipeline, committed backlog represents contractual obligations from paying customers. If even 50% converts at the expected pace, Nebius achieves $7B+ ARR.
B3

MOAT
European enterprises under GDPR and NIS2 require EU-based AI cloud infrastructure — Nebius is uniquely positioned as the only NVIDIA-backed EU cloud alternative to US hyperscalers. Regulatory-driven demand independent of competitive price wars is the most durable demand driver.
Why Institutions Avoid
A1
$7-9B ARR Target from $300M Run-Rate — 45x Scaling Risk
EXECUTION RISK
Scaling from $300M ARR (H1 2025 annualized) to $7-9B ARR by end-2026 requires 45-58x revenue growth in 18 months. This is the most aggressive growth target of any company on this watchlist. No company has achieved this scaling rate in cloud infrastructure history. The base case is meaningful, but institutional risk managers require acknowledging the execution probability is below 50%.
A2
Former Yandex Association — Institutional ESG Constraint
ESG
Despite complete separation from Russian operations, some institutional ESG mandates and geopolitical restriction policies automatically flag former Yandex entities. Sovereign wealth funds from NATO-allied countries and some US pension funds maintain explicit policies against former Russian tech company holdings regardless of current domicile or management.

Investment Thesis
Long-Term Investment Thesis · NBIS · Portfolio Manager Perspective
Nebius is a high-risk, high-reward institutional bet on European AI cloud infrastructure with the world's best possible strategic anchor: NVIDIA's $2B investment. The $20B backlog and EU data sovereignty requirements create a credible demand foundation. The $7-9B ARR target is binary: achieve it and the stock is dramatically undervalued at $28; miss it materially and the stock is overvalued. For institutions with explicit mandate space for speculative growth, NBIS offers a 3-5x potential return with commensurate risk.
