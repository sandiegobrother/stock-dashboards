Each ROAS improvement creates a positive feedback loop: better results → more spend → better data → even better results.

Industry Growth Context
The global digital advertising market is growing at 12% CAGR from $580B (2024) toward $740B (2028). Meta is growing faster than the market because AI-powered targeting is capturing budget from lower-ROI channels (TV, print, search). WhatsApp monetization represents a market expansion beyond the current digital ad market — adding commerce and messaging revenue. The $115B annual AI capex investment is creating a compounding advertising advantage that competitors cannot match without similar scale.

Growth Verdict
Growth Potential Verdict · META · +14% → +18% Est. Revenue Growth 2-4 Years
Meta's growth trajectory is solid and compounding — 12-14% annual revenue growth may appear modest for a tech company, but on a $164B revenue base it represents $20-25B of new revenue annually. The $115B AI capex investment is building advertising superiority that will compound for 5-10 years. The most exciting growth driver is the convergence of WhatsApp monetization + AI assistant subscription + AR glasses into a new platform-level revenue stream by 2027-2028. If Meta AI's 3.5B MAU converts even 5% to a paid subscription at $9.99/month, that's $21B in incremental annual revenue.
