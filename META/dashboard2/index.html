Revenue Analysis
Meta's revenue story is one of resilience through crisis. The 2022 decline triggered the 'Year of Efficiency' — 25%+ headcount reduction that restructured the cost base. Revenue fully recovered and accelerated: $134.9B (2023), $164.5B (2024), ~$187B (2025). The 2022 crisis became the catalyst for extraordinary 2023-2025 margin expansion.

Meta's net income grew from $23.2B (2022 trough) to $62.4B (2024) — a 169% increase in two years. The operating leverage from the Year of Efficiency permanently restructured Meta's cost base, enabling margin expansion that persists even as revenue grows.
