P/E Assessment
Meta's 25x forward P/E is in line with the large-cap internet sector average, but significantly undervalues the quality of the business. At $52B in FCF and 82% gross margins, Meta is arguably the highest-quality advertising business ever created. The 2022 trough at 11x P/E was an extraordinary buying opportunity. Today at 25x, the market is paying only a modest premium for: 3.35B daily active people, the most accurate advertising targeting platform in history, and Llama AI models reducing R&D cost. The $115-135B annual AI capex investment is the key P/E suppressor — markets are applying a 'show me' discount until ROI is demonstrated.
