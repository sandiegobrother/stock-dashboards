Analyst Verdict
Bottom Line · HOLD · Target $398.38
Tesla is the most polarizing stock in global markets — simultaneously a beaten-down car company (-3% revenue growth in 2025, BYD surpassing it globally) and a high-conviction AI/robotics option. The energy storage business (Megapack) is a genuine, fast-growing independent business that deserves credit. The brand damage from Musk's political associations is real and quantifiable. At 85x forward P/E, the stock prices in robotaxi with near-certainty — a binary bet that makes Tesla unsuitable as a core holding without deep conviction in the autonomous vehicle thesis. At the median analyst target of $398 there is 61% upside, but the range from $55 (bear) to $600 (bull) is the widest of any S&P 500 stock. Hold. Target: $398.38. Conviction required on the robotaxi/Optimus thesis for any outperformance.
