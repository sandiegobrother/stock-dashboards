P/E Assessment
Tesla's ~130x forward P/E is 7x the automotive sector average of 18x — the stock is not priced on current automotive earnings but entirely on Robotaxi, Optimus, and FSD optionality. With auto gross margins at 17% (down from 29% in 2021) and deliveries declining -8.6% in FY2025, the P/E multiple cannot be justified by the core auto business. The EV/EBITDA of 65x vs the auto sector's 4x illustrates the extraordinary optionality premium. This is not a traditional automotive valuation — Tesla requires $5-10B in annual Robotaxi/FSD revenue by 2027-2028 to justify current prices.
