Growth Potential Verdict · CRWV · +150% → +100% Est. Revenue Growth 2-4 Years
CoreWeave's growth potential is very high but levered. Revenue growing from $2.1B to $13B+ (520% cumulative in 3 years) driven by backlog conversion and customer diversification.
