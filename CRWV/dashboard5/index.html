#1
Microsoft Revenue Concentration (62%)
Competition
CRITICAL
9/10
40%
Microsoft represents approximately 62% of CoreWeave's revenue — an extraordinary concentration for a public company. Microsoft has been building its own Azure AI infrastructure at massive scale. Any reduction in Microsoft's GPU rental from CoreWeave — due to Azure capacity build-out, renegotiated terms, or strategic shift — would be catastrophic for CoreWeave's revenue trajectory.
Mitigant: Microsoft's $10B+ OpenAI investment and continued AI scaling require more GPU capacity than Azure can build alone in the near term. Multi-year contracted arrangements provide revenue floor.
#2
$7B+ Debt Service at High GPU Utilization Dependency
Financial
CRITICAL
9/10
35%
CoreWeave's $7B+ debt requires near-100% GPU utilization rates to service comfortably. If AI infrastructure demand softens, utilization drops, or NVIDIA releases next-generation GPUs making H100s less competitive, CoreWeave's cash flow coverage of debt service deteriorates rapidly. The 2026 Blackwell transition could create a gap where H100 clusters underutilize.
Mitigant: $25B+ backlog provides multi-year committed revenue that ensures utilization floor. NVIDIA partnership provides preferential Blackwell upgrade access.
#3
GPU Hardware Obsolescence Cycle
Industry Disruption
HIGH
8/10
50%
NVIDIA releases new GPU architectures annually (H100 → H200 → Blackwell → Vera Rubin). CoreWeave's $7B+ debt is collateralized by depreciating GPU hardware. As each new generation arrives, prior-generation GPU rental rates decline. The H100 → Blackwell transition in 2025-2026 is already compressing H100 pricing.
Mitigant: CoreWeave is actively upgrading to Blackwell through NVIDIA's preferred customer program. Long-term contracts are priced to include hardware refresh economics.
#4
Customer Diversification Failure
Economic
HIGH
7/10
45%
Beyond Microsoft, CoreWeave needs to demonstrate genuine customer diversification to justify public company valuations. OpenAI, Cohere, and Stability AI are customers — but none approach the scale of Microsoft. If CoreWeave cannot reduce Microsoft concentration below 40% by FY2027, the re-rating thesis is constrained.
Mitigant: IPO proceeds and public company profile increase CoreWeave's enterprise sales credibility. AWS and Google Cloud customers increasingly diversify AI workloads to avoid hyperscaler lock-in.
#5
Interest Rate Sensitivity on $7B+ Float-Rate Debt
Economic
MEDIUM
6/10
30%
A significant portion of CoreWeave's debt is floating-rate — each 100bps increase in rates adds ~$70M in annual interest expense. In a sustained high-rate environment, FCF is materially impaired.
Mitigant: CoreWeave has been refinancing toward fixed-rate instruments. Revenue growth at 230%+ provides rapid cash flow scaling that absorbs rate increases.
