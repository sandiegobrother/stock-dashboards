MRD Testing — Molecular Residual Disease
New Products
VERY HIGH
Minimal Residual Disease (MRD) testing detects cancer recurrence at the molecular level — before imaging can identify it. Q4 2025 MRD testing volume grew +56% QoQ, making it Tempus's fastest-growing segment. MRD testing requires both sequencing (Tempus) and AI interpretation (Tempus) — a vertically integrated offering competitors can't easily match.

Pharma Data Licensing — 40+ Partners
AI Technology
HIGH
Tempus licenses its 7M+ patient dataset to 40+ pharmaceutical companies for clinical trial matching, drug discovery, and biomarker identification.
