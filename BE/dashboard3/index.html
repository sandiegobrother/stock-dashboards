3 · Switching Costs
Switching Costs · Score 7/10
7
Once a Bloom Energy system is installed on a data center campus, switching costs are extremely high. Replacing an on-site Bloom fuel cell with alternative power would require either new grid connection (6-18 months, $50M+ per data center), backup generators (less reliable, less clean), or competing fuel cell installation — all requiring significant capital and planning, with unacceptable downtime risk.
