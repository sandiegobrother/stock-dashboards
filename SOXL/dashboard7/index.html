Why Institutions Avoid
A1
NOT AN INVESTMENT — Tactical Instrument Only
FUNDAMENTAL
SOXL is a daily-rebalanced leveraged ETF designed for short-term directional trading — it is not a company and should never be held as a long-term investment. Institutional investors who hold SOXL for months or years are misusing the instrument. The fund explicitly states it seeks daily (not long-term) 3x returns.
A2
-82% in 2022 — Requires +456% to Recover
RISK
SOXL lost 82% of its value in 2022 when SOX fell 36% — a -46 percentage point amplification. Recovery from -82% required a 456% gain (which SOXL subsequently delivered through 2023-2024). This illustrates the structural danger: large losses require proportionally much larger gains to recover, and the recovery can take years.

COSTS
