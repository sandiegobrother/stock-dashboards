Network Effects · Score 9/10

3 · Switching Costs
Switching Costs · Score 8/10
8
AppLovin's SDK is deeply embedded in game codebases — replacing it requires developer time, A/B testing, and performance uncertainty. Advertisers who have optimized campaigns in AppLovin's platform have spent months tuning creative, audience segments, and bid strategies — migrating to ironSource or Digital Turbine means starting from scratch with lower initial performance.
